The Bazaar is the place where you will find carefully crafted designs, templates, and add-ons guarantying you a website that makes a difference, for a fraction of the price of a website build from scratch.

To place an order, follow this steps:

  • Browse the catalogue to choose the presets
  • Fill out the pricing form and press 'Place order'
  • Create a free account on Fiverr
  • Message the Front-End Bazaar profile page, mentioning the passcode you entered

Then, I will create a custom order for you for the price you received upon completing the Pricing form and get to work.

However, if you do not find any of the presets appealing, follow the right path to order a website from scratch.

Note: The building process is not automatic. Also, the QA and the Responsive Design tests are done 100% manually in order to guarantee the best possible quality.
